Evolve against Claude — the user in the loop

Claude plays a normal user: it poses a real question and EOReader answers it by running a real turn — it searches the live web, folds every page it keeps, and renders a grounded answer (exactly the reader's pipeline). Claude then scores how well that answer turned the pages it retrieved into grounded, flowing output — never fact-checked against Claude's own knowledge. That score is what the population evolves toward. Claude grades; it never touches a weight, and it never answers its own question.
Evolution goes as deep as the body; judgement stays with you.
The deeper mechanics — the freeze boundary, Void-respect, and the transfer probe — live in docs/organ-level-evolution.md.
