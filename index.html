What makes dolphins intelligent?
Surface-smoothing is the last layer. Prose summarization stays locked until every cited claim is Supported. Right now 1 of 4 is Contested — organize the evidence first; the summary comes after.
Dolphin intelligence is best read as several partly-independent capacities rather than one trait. Relative brain size is unusually high, individuals recognise themselves in mirrors, and foraging techniques pass between generations as culture. The strongest popular claim — that signature whistles are true names — is only partly supported and remains actively contested in the literature.
