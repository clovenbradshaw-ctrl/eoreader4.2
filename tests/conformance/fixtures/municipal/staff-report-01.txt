CITY OF FAIRVIEW HEIGHTS
STAFF REPORT TO THE CITY COUNCIL

TO: Mayor Patricia Owusu and Members of the City Council
FROM: Carla Fenwick, Director of Public Works
DATE: April 8, 2025
SUBJECT: Recommendation to Award Contract for the Birchwood Park Renovation

RECOMMENDATION

Staff recommends that the Council award the contract for the Birchwood Park Renovation Project to Halden Construction in the amount of $612,450, and authorize the City Manager to execute the agreement.

BACKGROUND

In January 2025, the Council directed staff to solicit bids for the renovation of Birchwood Park, including replacement of the playground equipment, resurfacing of the basketball courts, and installation of a new irrigation system. The Purchasing Division, under Deputy Director Omar Sadiq, issued Request for Proposals 25-07 on February 3, 2025. Three firms submitted proposals by the March 1 deadline: Halden Construction, Meridian Site Works, and Torbay Contractors.

A review panel consisting of Sadiq, Fenwick, and Parks Superintendent Yolanda Beck evaluated the proposals against the criteria published in the RFP, including price, prior experience with municipal park projects, and proposed schedule. The panel scored Halden Construction highest overall. Beck noted that Halden had completed similar work for the neighboring city of Ashcombe with favorable references.

FISCAL IMPACT

The Birchwood Park Renovation was budgeted at $650,000 in the Fiscal Year 2025 Capital Improvement Program. Halden Construction's proposal of $612,450 is within the budgeted amount. Sadiq confirmed that sufficient funds remain in the capital account after accounting for the Elm Street change order approved by the Council in March.

TIMELINE

Fenwick told the panel that, if awarded, Halden Construction expected to begin mobilization in May 2025 and complete the project by September 2025, ahead of the fall soccer season.
