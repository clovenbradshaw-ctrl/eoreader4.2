BRIEF: FAIRVIEW HEIGHTS PLANNING COMMISSION GAINS NEW MEMBER

FAIRVIEW HEIGHTS — The City Council on Tuesday confirmed the appointment of civil engineer Louis Marchetti to the Planning Commission, filling a seat that had been vacant since January.

Mayor Patricia Owusu recommended Marchetti, citing his twenty years of engineering experience. The appointment passed 4-0.

Marchetti will serve a three-year term. The Planning Commission next meets April 21.
