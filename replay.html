Replay — watching something get read

No ingest organ returns an answer. It returns a distribution. The collapse — the choice of one word — happens at read time, against a corpus you can switch on and off, and it can be run again, differently, without the model ever running twice. Turn a source off and watch a word change. The transcript was never a fact about the audio. It was a fact about the audio and the corpus together.
